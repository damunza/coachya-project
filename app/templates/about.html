COACHYA

About Coachya

This application was designed with an aim of connecting teams to coaches.This arose after we discovered that most tems were stuggling to develop and we thought of creating a platform for them to acquire aid whether financial,counseling or skill development


How it Works

The app allows users to view teams and coaches. When users log in to the application they are able to create a profile either as a team or as a coach. Coaches are then allowed to send messages to the teams to contact them if they are interested in the team. The teams are alloewed to view their profile where they can read messages sent to their profile. Upon viewing the messages they acquire the email of the coach interested in them and from there they can communicate via email
